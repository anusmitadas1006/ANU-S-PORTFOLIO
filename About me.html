About Me

I am a passionate and aspiring web designer currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at Techno International Newtown. With a strong foundation in programming and design, I am dedicated to crafting visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I have a keen eye for aesthetics, usability, and modern web trends, and I am constantly learning new technologies to enhance my skills. My goal is to create engaging digital experiences that blend creativity with functionality, making the web more accessible and intuitive for users.

As a BCA student, my approach to the web design process is a blend of creativity, technical proficiency, and user-centered thinking. I start by understanding the project requirements and target audience, ensuring that the design aligns with user needs and business goals. I focus on wireframing and prototyping to structure layouts effectively before moving on to coding with clean, responsive, and optimized designs. Leveraging my knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design principles, I prioritize aesthetics, functionality, and performance. I also believe in continuous learning and iteration, refining my designs based on feedback and the latest industry trends to create visually appealing and seamless web experiences.
